College Choice Tool: Size

After 5 minutes, the survey will automatically move to the next question.


Every college and university ranges in amount of students. Some schools have less than 3,000 students, whereas others have more than 60,000. While some prefer a large student body, others prefer a smaller one. Both styles offer opportunities for community building, therefore the decision is completely up to your preference. If you would prefer a compromise between these options, feel free to click medium.
